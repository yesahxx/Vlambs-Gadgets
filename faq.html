Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our products, services, shipping, returns, and more. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact our customer support team.

Shopping & Orders

How do I place an order?

+

Placing an order at VLAMB is easy! Browse our products, select the item you want to purchase, choose any additional specifications or options, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Can I modify or cancel my order after it's placed?

+

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer service team. After this window, orders typically enter processing and cannot be modified. For urgent cases, please contact us immediately and we'll do our best to accommodate your request.

Do you offer international shipping?

+

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary depending on your location. You can view shipping options and costs during checkout before finalizing your purchase. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the customer.

How can I track my order?

+

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your VLAMB account and viewing your order history. Our tracking system provides regular updates on your package's location and estimated delivery date.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for certain regions. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway to ensure your financial information remains safe.

Do you offer price matching?

+

Yes, we offer price matching on identical products found at lower prices from authorized retailers. To request a price match, contact our customer service team with a link to the competitor's product page showing the lower price. Price match requests must be made before purchase and are subject to verification.

Are there any hidden fees?

+

No, we don't believe in hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay, plus any applicable taxes and shipping costs that are clearly displayed during checkout. For international orders, please be aware that customs duties and import taxes may apply according to your country's regulations.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for most products. Items must be returned in their original packaging, in new or like-new condition. To initiate a return, log in to your account and select the order you wish to return, or contact our customer service team. Once your return is approved, you'll receive instructions on how to send the item back.

How long does the warranty period last?

+

Warranty periods vary by product: smartphones and laptops come with a 24-month warranty, cameras with an 18-month warranty, and accessories with a 12-month warranty. Our warranties cover manufacturing defects and hardware failures that occur during normal use. For detailed warranty information, please check the product specification page or contact customer service.

What happens if I receive a defective product?

+

If you receive a defective product, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery with photos or videos documenting the issue. We'll arrange for a replacement or repair under warranty. For items damaged during shipping, please document the damage before opening the package fully, as this will help with the claims process.

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

+

Creating an account is simple. Click the "Sign In" button at the top of our website, then select "Create Account." You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and enter some basic information. Alternatively, you can use your Google, Facebook, or Apple account for a quicker setup.

How is my personal information protected?

+

We take data protection seriously and comply with global privacy regulations. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your data to third parties and only use it to process orders and improve your shopping experience. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

What should I do if I forgot my password?

+

If you've forgotten your password, click the "Sign In" button, then select "Forgot Password." Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours.

Still have questions?

Our customer support team is here to help. Feel free to reach out if you couldn't find the answer to your question.
